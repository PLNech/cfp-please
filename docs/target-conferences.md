### 2. ECIR (European Conference on Information Retrieval)
- **Website:** https://ecir2026.eu/
- **CFP Full Papers:** https://ecir2026.eu/calls/call-for-full-papers
- **CFP Short Papers:** https://ecir2026.eu/calls/call-for-short-papers
- **2026 Dates:** March 30 - April 1, 2026
- **Location:** Delft, Netherlands
- **Deadlines:**
  - Full Papers: Abstract Sept 25, Paper Oct 2, 2025
  - Short Papers: Abstract Oct 7, Paper Oct 14, 2025
  - Notifications: December 16, 2025
- **Topics:** Search, ranking, recommender systems, conversational search, ML for IR, RAG, LLMs

### 4. ai.engineer (AI Engineer World's Fair)
- **Website:** https://www.ai.engineer/worldsfair

- **Location:** San Francisco Marriott Marquis, SF
- **Scale:** 20 tracks, 250 speakers, 6,000 attendees
- **Contact:** sponsorships@ai.engineer
